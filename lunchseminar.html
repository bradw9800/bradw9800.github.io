Oxford London Gauge Assembly (OLGA)


The LSGNT lunch seminar is held every Tuesday from 12:00-14:00 in the room MB1.2, Macadam building, KCL. It is a great opportunity for the first year students to meet potential supervisors and get a taste of some research level maths.
